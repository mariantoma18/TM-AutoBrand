TM Motors

Crafted in Romania. Designed for the World.

TM Motors was born from a bold vision — to redefine Romania’s presence in the global automotive industry. Founded in 2020, our brand blends engineering precision, intuitive design, and a strong identity rooted in local pride and global ambition.

Elyssion — the refined city sedan — and Teraon — the versatile, commanding SUV — are the first expressions of this new standard in mobility. Every line, every feature, and every innovation reflects our commitment to comfort, technology, and sustainability.

With intelligent systems, customizable interiors, and efficient engines, TM Motors builds more than just cars — we build a movement. One that begins in Romania and moves confidently toward the world, one model at a time.
