Crafted in Romania. Designed for the World.

In 2020, a vision was born — one that dared to challenge conventions and reimagine the Romanian automotive identity. With passion, engineering precision, and bold creativity, TM Motors took its first steps toward becoming a symbol of modern mobility.

Our mission was simple yet ambitious: to build vehicles that transcend trends, embody timeless elegance, and empower the driver at every turn. And now, five years later, that mission becomes reality.

From the hills of Transylvania to the Black Sea, Romania has long been a land of builders, inventors, and dreamers. TM Motors carries that spirit forward — fueled by a new generation of engineers and designers who believe that global excellence can be crafted at home.

Our story began with more than an idea — it began with purpose. Every decision we make, from the drawing board to the test track, reflects our dedication to redefining what a Romanian-made vehicle can be in the eyes of the world.

2025 — Our First Chapter Begins

This year marks the launch of our debut models — Elyssion, the refined sedan designed for urban sophistication, and Teraon, the bold SUV engineered for every journey. Each line, each surface, each innovation speaks to our design philosophy: intelligent, intuitive, and inherently human.

These are more than vehicles. They are the beginning of a new era in Romanian automotive excellence.

The launch of Elyssion and Teraon is not only a technological milestone — it's the realization of years of planning, prototyping, and refinement. These two models are the voice of a brand that believes in progress, elegance, and accessibility.

Designed, engineered, and assembled with care, these vehicles prove that premium automotive design doesn’t need to come from outside — it can start here, in Romania.

Elyssion — Elegance in Motion

Elyssion is the definition of balance — between form and function, performance and comfort. Its sculpted silhouette glides effortlessly through city streets, while its luxurious interior offers a serene space shaped by your preferences. Available in a range of curated exterior colors and customizable interiors, Elyssion is a statement of personality.

Whether powered by our Petrol 2.0 TSX or Diesel 2.0 TDX engine, every Elyssion delivers refined dynamics and responsive power. A sedan crafted for those who expect more from every drive.

Inside the Elyssion, drivers will find a space that merges contemporary luxury with cutting-edge comfort. Ambient lighting, customizable displays, and intelligent climate control systems respond to your every move. It’s not just a car — it’s a curated experience.

Sound insulation, ergonomic seating, and soft-touch surfaces create a tranquil environment, whether you're commuting to work or escaping the city on a weekend drive. Elyssion is serenity in motion — understated on the outside, unforgettable on the inside.

Teraon — Presence. Performance. Possibility.

Teraon was built to command attention. With a bold exterior stance, spacious interior, and smart technology, it's more than an SUV — it's an invitation to explore. Every journey becomes a story, whether you're navigating urban streets or venturing beyond the city.

Designed for versatility and strength, powered by the same proven technology as Elyssion, Teraon blends elegance and capability into a truly Romanian performance vehicle.

Teraon is made for those who demand more — more space, more freedom, more confidence. Its high ground clearance, adaptive suspension, and intelligent drive modes make it as capable on a forest trail as it is in urban traffic.

Smart loading zones, wireless charging, and modular storage enhance every trip. Whether you're carrying passengers, gear, or ambition — Teraon is ready to adapt.

Innovation that Listens

At TM Motors, innovation is not about complexity — it's about clarity. From adaptive driver-assist features to real-time diagnostics and connected interfaces, our technology is engineered to enhance your driving experience, not overwhelm it.

Every vehicle is crafted with a sense of anticipation — understanding what you need before you ask for it.

We believe innovation should anticipate, not intimidate. That’s why we’ve built an ecosystem where voice control, predictive navigation, and AI-powered suggestions adapt to your routines, offering assistance exactly when it’s needed.

Over-the-air updates ensure your TM Motors vehicle continues to improve over time, with security patches, feature upgrades, and system enhancements delivered remotely and safely.

Responsibility in Every Detail

Sustainability is not a feature — it’s a foundation. Our production facilities are built with energy efficiency in mind, our materials are sourced responsibly, and we are committed to reducing emissions across our entire value chain.

We believe that luxury and responsibility must go hand in hand. That’s why our future includes not just hybrids — but fully electric vehicles designed with the same soul as our originals.

Our hybrid platforms already reduce emissions significantly, and we are actively developing a fully electric architecture to lead the charge into a greener future. From paint to packaging, every element of our production pipeline is scrutinized for its impact — and improved accordingly.

Partnering with local suppliers and renewable energy providers, TM Motors is building a future that’s not only intelligent — but responsible.

Looking Forward

TM Motors is more than a brand. It’s a statement — of confidence, identity, and forward motion. We are just at the beginning of our journey, and the road ahead holds even more boldness, beauty, and breakthrough engineering.

From Romania to the world, we invite you to join a new kind of driving experience. One that's designed with heart, built with purpose, and driven by tomorrow.

In the coming years, our roadmap includes full electrification, autonomous driving support, and a suite of digital services designed to connect car, user, and world. We are investing in infrastructure, expanding our network of service partners, and building a new research campus in Brașov.

With every new milestone, we’re driven by the same principle that started it all: mobility is not just about moving — it’s about moving forward with intention, innovation, and integrity.
